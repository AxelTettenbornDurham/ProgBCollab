Smart Fridge

What is a smart fridge

They are fridges connected to the internet and often have an attached app

They allow you to control the temperature, operate at the most efficient energy levels, tell you if there are any problems or faults and tell you what items you have in the fridge (including when items go out of date, when you need to buy more items and what in-date items are currently in the fridge)
